Tips for Commuting with the LRT


How to Purchase Tickets

    Find the ticket vending machine or booth at any station.
    Pick either a single-journey ticket or load your Beep card.
    For Beep cards, you can tap them at the turnstile to access them, and you can use them until your balance runs out.
    Using a Beep card saves time and ensures quicker entry and exit through busy stations, making it a better option for daily riders.

Operating Hours

    The Manila LRT-1 operates from 4:30 AM to 10:00 PM, Monday through Sunday.

    Expect some changes in operation hours during public holidays or major city events.
    Early risers and late travelers should plan their journeys carefully to avoid missing the last train.

Make Your Journey Hassle-Free and Stress-Free

    Use Commuter Apps like Google Maps to map out your LRT-1 ride
    Reload your card in advance to avoid long queues
    Travel during Off-Peak Hours to see fewer passengers
    Mind Your Belongings

Customer Etiquette Tips

    Always queue properly when boarding or waiting for trains.
    Prioritise designated seats for the elderly, pregnant women, and PWDs
    Avoid blocking doors to ensure easier and faster boarding for everyone.
